What is Smoke Control CFD Simulation?

Learn about Computational Fluid Dynamics and how SmokeSim applies it for smoke control

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) for smoke control uses advanced numerical methods to model fluid flow, heat transfer, and combustion in fire scenarios. Our expertise centers on NIST's Fire Dynamics Simulator (FDS) — the world's leading fire modeling software.

🔥 Fire Dynamics Modeling

Advanced FDS CFD simulation for accurate fire and smoke behavior prediction and analysis using validated modeling protocols.

💨 Smoke Control CFD

Comprehensive smoke ventilation CFD analysis including visibility predictions, temperature mapping, and time-to-tenability calculations.

📊 CFD Analysis Outputs

Detailed CFD simulation results: 3D visualizations, temperature contours, smoke layer heights, and extraction efficiency analysis.

📋 Regulatory Compliance

Professional CFD reports formatted for building control approval, compliant with UK fire safety regulations and international standards.
