What is CFD?

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) uses numerical methods to model fluid flow, heat transfer and combustion. At SmokeSim we apply NIST's Fire Dynamics Simulator (FDS) using validated modelling protocols and sensitivity analysis. Typical outputs include visibility and temperature predictions, time‑to‑tenability, detailed visualisations, model files and a regulator‑ready technical report with design recommendations and uncertainty notes.
